Identity and site design development

The Bionum is a service that tells how ordinary household objects affect the environment and human life.

Graphics

The difference between the Bionum and the rest of the publications about ecology is simplicity and science. Therefore, the corporate graphics and logo show the fusion of science and simplicity. Objects are the basis of graphics and stand out well in the context of all visual content.

Main page

On the main page you can find any item and read about it. Also, on the home page you can find interesting content in the corporate style of the Bionum. Thus, people are immersed in the theme.

Articles

There are in the Bionum all of usual household items. Each subject has its own article about its impact on the human and environment. Anyone can add a new subject and write an article to it. And the editorial staff will check everything and release it.
