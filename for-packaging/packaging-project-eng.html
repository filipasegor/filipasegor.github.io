Trimas and Zolushka Metallist produce and sell sausages, salami, semi-finished products and other meat products. Labels have been designed for their products.

Graphics

The main objective of the graphics is to show that the products are made for those who appreciate and love meat. That’s why rock paintings, which refer to the traditions of our ancestors, are taken as a basis. Such graphics distinguish the product from the rest and associate it with the brand.

Illustration

A separate illustration is selected For each package. It corresponds to the type of meat the product is made of. Thus, branded graphics helps buyers to choose the appropriate option.

In life

Branded graphics can be used not only on the packaging itself. It is distributed and fits well on all media of the company.
