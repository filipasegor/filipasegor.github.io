Identity and logotype design

"Coffee and people" are a cozy coffee shop in downtown Samara. This is a place where strangers often gather to drink delicious coffee, meet new people and distract themselves from their everyday problems.

The concept

The concept of a coffee shop is to bring strangers together in one place and introduce them to each other. It is a place where no politics, violence and everyday life problems. There are only delicious coffee and good people.

People call

The coffeehouse’s signature style is people. On the streets and public spaces, they encourage other people to break away from their everyday problems and listen to each other, get acquainted, drink coffee.
