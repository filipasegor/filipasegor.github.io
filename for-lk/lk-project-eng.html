Personal cabinet

Interface and promo page design

The company NEO-Bureau is engaged in an independent estimation and expertise of all kinds of property. Personal cabinet is their service, in which bureau’s partners leave requests for necessary services and receive rewards for it.

Limitations

Personal cabinet is a new product on the market of assessment and expertise. And only time can make sure that it is profitable. Therefore, the functionality of the product had to be reduced to financial limitations.

Creation of applications

In your personal cabinet you can easily and quickly leave requests for the necessary services, upload necessary photos and documents and enter customer data.

My applications

All created requests are in one place. It is easy to monitor them and check their readiness. You can also add new files.

Mobility

Many of the partners of NEO-Bureaux often make deals outside their offices. Therefore, the interface of their personal cabinet is designed to be convenient for them to use on a smartphone.

Promo page

Also, the page was designed, where it is clear about all the advantages of a personal cabinet.
